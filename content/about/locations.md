## 2026 Event: March 27–29, 2026

Texas Bible Bowl runs **two simultaneous locations** with identical competition material.
Choose the location that best suits your travel plans.

---

## Texas Bible Bowl South

**Bandina Christian Youth Camp**
320 Bandina Ranch Rd, Bandera, TX 78003
*Texas Hill Country*

<iframe src="https://www.google.com/maps?q=320+Bandina+Ranch+Rd,+Bandera,+TX+78003&output=embed" width="100%" height="300" style="border:0;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem;" allowfullscreen loading="lazy"></iframe>

[Download Camp Map](/uploads/2/8/7/6/28767859/bcyc_map_2026.pdf)

**What to Bring:**
- ESV Bible (required for testing)
- Bedding (sleeping bag, pillow, towels)
- Clothing (casual wear, pajamas, extra socks and underwear)
- Footwear (tennis shoes, shower shoes)
- Personal hygiene items and flashlight
- Refillable water bottle, sunscreen, bug spray, umbrella

## Texas Bible Bowl West

**White River Youth Camp**
2838 FM 2794, Spur, TX 79370
*Approximately 45 minutes southeast of Lubbock*

<iframe src="https://www.google.com/maps?q=2838+FM+2794,+Spur,+TX+79370&output=embed" width="100%" height="300" style="border:0;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem;" allowfullscreen loading="lazy"></iframe>

**What to Bring:**
- ESV Bible (required for testing)
- Bedding for twin bunks (sleeping bag or sheets + pillow)
- Personal hygiene items (toiletries, towel, shower shoes)
- Clothing (tennis shoes, changes of clothes, pajamas)
- Flashlight, refillable water bottle with a secure lid
- Sunscreen, bug spray, umbrella
